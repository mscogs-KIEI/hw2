About Scogs' Rocks and Roll

That photo of the guy enjoying a concert? That's me!

During quarantine, I realized what I missed most in life: loud music, bouldering, and community spaces. After receiving a Kellogg MBA, I returned home to Greenpoint to open my dream business venture.

Our Mission is to be an inclusive, safe venue for bouldering enthusiasts and music lovers. From metal yoga, to outdoors hiking trips, we promise you'll find activities and a community of people you'll love here.
